# 🛡️ Aegis-Pathfinder Nexus
### Sovereign AI Governance & Career Intelligence Ecosystem

**Nexus-Aegis** is a dual-purpose AI platform built for the 2026 compliance era. It solves the two biggest problems in modern tech: **Professional Growth** and **Algorithmic Trust.**

---

## 🚀 Core Components

### 1. 🧠 Career Pathfinder (The Worker)
* **Purpose:** Transforms raw professional data into actionable career trajectories.
* **Logic:** Uses AI-driven skill-gap analysis to identify "power-up" skills (like AI Ethics) that unlock high-value roles.
* **Outcome:** A real-time career GPS that adapts to market shifts.

### 2. 🚦 Aegis Sentinel (The Boss)
* **Purpose:** The "Conscience of the Machine." 
* **Logic:** A real-time governance dashboard that audits AI outputs against the **EU AI Act**, **NIST RMF**, and **GDPR**.
* **Outcome:** Continuous risk scoring and bias detection to ensure AI stays safe, legal, and ethical.

### 3. 🎨 Imagination Studio (The Creator)
* **Purpose:** Multi-modal generative AI.
* **Logic:** Transforms career visions into creative stories and visual assets.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack (Zero-Cost / High-Impact)

* **Compliance:** Integrated EU AI Act & NIST Framework logic
